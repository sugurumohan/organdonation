What can you donate?

Types of organ donation

Everyone has a choice as to whether or not they want to become an organ donor after they die, and if you choose to

donate, you can choose to donate either some or all of your organs.

By registering to become an organ donor you have the option to donate organs such as your heart, lungs, liver, kidneys,

pancreas and small bowel. All of these forms of donation can greatly enhance or even save the life of someone in need.
